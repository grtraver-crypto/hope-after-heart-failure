Put your real images in this folder, then update index.html to use them.

Suggested files (names are up to you):
  - book-cover.png   (the 3D book cover in the hero + "Get the Book" section)
  - author.jpg       (Greg Traver portrait in the author section)
  - community.jpg    (the two women on the bench, "You Are Not Alone")

HOW TO SWAP A PLACEHOLDER FOR A REAL IMAGE
------------------------------------------
1. Save the image into this /images folder.
2. Open index.html and find the matching placeholder block, e.g.:

       <div class="book-placeholder">...</div>

   Replace it with:

       <img src="images/book-cover.png" alt="Hope After Heart Failure book cover" />

3. Save. The image will appear where the placeholder was.
